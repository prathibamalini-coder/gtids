Our Services

A complete ecosystem for inclusive finance and livelihoods.

Our services go beyond basic banking access — focusing on sustained usage, economic participation, and long-term impact.

01
Doorstep banking access

Financial Inclusion Services

We enable seamless access to formal banking for rural and tribal populations by facilitating zero-balance account opening, Aadhaar-enabled services, deposits, withdrawals, remittances, and balance inquiries through our Bank Mitra network.

02
Transparent welfare delivery

Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) Enablement

GTIDS ensures beneficiaries receive government entitlements directly into their bank accounts in a timely and transparent manner, reducing leakages and improving access to welfare schemes.

03
Knowledge for the last mile

Financial Literacy & Awareness

We conduct community-level training and awareness programs to build financial knowledge, promote savings habits, and encourage responsible use of financial services.

04
Capital for entrepreneurs

Credit Linkages & Microfinance

We connect individuals and small entrepreneurs to formal credit systems, enabling access to microfinance, small loans, and financial products that support income generation and business growth.

05
Building household resilience

Savings & Investment Promotion

GTIDS encourages a culture of savings by facilitating recurring deposits, fixed deposits, and other financial instruments that help households build financial security.

06
Banking + income, together

Livelihood & Skilling Integration

Recognizing that financial inclusion must be linked to income, we integrate banking services with skilling programs and livelihood opportunities in collaboration with Centurion University of Technology and Management.

07
Trust, built locally

Last-Mile Service Delivery Network

Our strong network of trained Bank Mitras ensures doorstep delivery of financial services, particularly in remote and underserved regions, building trust and accessibility within communities.

08
Scaling through collaboration

Institutional Partnerships

We work closely with public sector banks, government agencies, and development institutions to deliver scalable and sustainable solutions for inclusive growth.

09
One card · full social security

Social Security Scheme – 1 Card (SSS)

A unified social security card initiative that consolidates access to government welfare entitlements — pensions, insurance and benefit schemes — into a single, easy-to-use card for rural and tribal households.
